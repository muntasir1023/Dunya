Our Story, Your Flavors

Bringing the finest international ingredients and authentic tastes from around the world to the heart of the Netherlands.

15+ Years of Quality

Rooted in Heritage, Dedicated to Quality

Dunya International Supermarket began with a simple mission: to provide our community in Nijmegen with the authentic flavors they grew up with, while introducing new, exotic tastes to local food enthusiasts.

What started as a small family-owned corner shop has grown into a premier destination for international groceries, fresh halal meats, and specialty imports from the Middle East, Asia, and Mediterranean regions.

We believe that food is the universal language of connection. Every product on our shelves is hand-selected for its quality and authenticity, ensuring you get the best experience in every bite.

Why Choose Dunya?

Our commitment to excellence drives everything we do.

Quality First

Community Centric

More Than Just a Supermarket
